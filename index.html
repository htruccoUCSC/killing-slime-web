About the Game

Slimes From Another Universe!

In Killing Slime, you the player are solely responsible for defending the timeline from invading slimes from another another universe. Defend the past, present, and future by fortifying your position with different versions of your chud friend from different timelines. Choose the right units, augments, items, and faction synergies to survive the onslaught of slimes.

Featuring!

Lane-Based Tower Defense

Place units along lanes to defend against waves of invading slimes.

Factions From Different Timelines

Choose from a variety of factions, each with unique abilities and synergies, representing different eras of time.

Augments

Every three rounds choose from a selection of powerful augments to enhance your units.

Chuds

A seemingly infinite army of your chud friend is at your disposal. They each have lived a different life, in a different time period, providing unique abilities and support.
